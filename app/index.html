Good Shepherd

Good Shepherd is a nationally recognized rehabilitation leader, offering a continuum of care for people with injuries, complex medical needs and physical and/or cognitive disabilities. Aligning with their mission to deliver a top-notch experience, Good Shepherd wanted a simple and convenient way to inform their patients and visitors about their facility and the surrounding city.

I created a beautiful app that puts everything patients and visitors need to know at their fingertips. Information is pulled from multiple websites and data sources to present up-to-date visiting hours, cafeteria menus, and local information, and an interactive map makes navigating Good Shepherd's impressive facility a breeze.

Westbury Friends School

Westbury Friends School needed a website that made it easy for parents of prospective students to learn about their programs, schedule a visit, and apply, all while keeping parents of current students informed and engaged.

The result was a beautiful website utilizing on-site photography, with easy-to-access information and events, and a simple application process (including taking application fees).
